Education Level and Income Distribution

library(ggplot2)
library(scales)
load("../data/ids_82.rda")
load("../data/inc_h_94.rda")
load("../data/ihs.rda")

ggplot(ihs, aes(x = PFYRealIncome, color = Educ3, fill = Educ3, group = Educ3)) + 
    geom_density(bw = "SJ", alpha = 0.2) + scale_x_log10(labels = comma) + facet_grid(Sex ~ 
    Year) + ggtitle("Annual earned income by education") + theme_bw(15)

Weighted density by coarse education level. Gaussian Kernel.
